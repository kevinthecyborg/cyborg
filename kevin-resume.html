About Me

I'm a twenty-eight year old designer and front-end web developer from Texas, currently based in Los Angeles. I love everything that has to do with computers and got into design via my highschool class 'Digital Graphics and Animation'.

I started freelancing at seventeen and have stuck with it ever since. I currently co-own a design and development company with my partner, Skyler Whittlesey, called cyborg. We exclusively build in-house products and launched our first iOS app 'InstaVid' in April of 2014.

Design is my number one passion and I picked up front-end development because I grew tired of other developers not being able to fully implement my designs. I really love being in full control of the UI/UX of a site or app.
