Business Grader

Our tool to grade projects we're building.

Basics

  • What is the idea?

  • What needs does it address?

  • What are the risks involved?

  • Who are your competitors?

  • What gives your product/team a competitive advantage?

Tech

  • What platform(s) is it for?

  • What architecture is it built on?

  • Will it have multi-language (spoken) support?

  • When will dev be completed?

Growth

  • What is your market potential?

  • Have you formed any alliances/partnerships?

  • What is the growth rate for your industry?

  • How will you acquire users?

  • How will you keep users?

  • When will your company break even?

  • How are you scaling the company tech wise?

  • How are you scaling the team to match the growth?

Exit

  • What are the probable exit scenarios?
