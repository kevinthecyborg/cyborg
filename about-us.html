About Us

Who we are, what we do, & why we do it.

cy·borg

/ˈsīˌbôrg/

noun

A person whose physical and mental abilities are extended beyond normal human limitations by technology aiding the body and mind.

The Future

Technology is increasing at an exponential rate.

Futurist and inventor Ray Kurzweil has predicted that, "Within a quarter century, nonbiological intelligence will match the range and subtlety of human intelligence." There is no denying that technology is starting to reach this exponential increase in density and complexity.

The Challenge

We believe this technology should be used to better humanity.

Just as an explosion can take a rocket to the moon or destroy a city, this technology can be used for good or evil. We stand to be at the forefront of innovation to ensure the future is guided by peaceful technology.

Our Vision

To create sufficiently advanced technology to push the human race forward.

Core Values
Create work that reflects your passion

Everyone has different reasons for doing what they do in order to survive. In today's society that translates to making money. We choose to only work alongside others that are passionate about using their skill-set to better humankind.

Innovate by always pursuing personal growth

The cliché saying, "a chain is only as strong as its weakest link" stuck around for a reason. It's true. We encourage everyone on the team to be dedicated to bettering themselves in every way possible.

Continue to find new outlets that inspire creativity

We live in an amazing, dynamic universe that is teeming with undiscovered patterns. Always stay curious.

Form honest relationships with open communication

Trust is something that must be earned. We believe in forming positive connections for positive results.

Maintain accountability by taking responsibility for what you say and do

If a commitment is made, it is your duty to see it through.
